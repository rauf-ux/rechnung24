# Product

## Problem

- **2027:** XRechnung becomes mandatory for all B2B invoices in Germany.
- **Existing tools** (Lexoffice, sevDesk) are full bookkeeping suites at €40+/month — overkill for solo freelancers.
- **Target users** need fast, simple invoicing without accounting overhead.

## Solution

XRechnung-conformant, mobile-first invoicing app with a 60-second workflow, ~4× cheaper than competitors, fully aligned with German tax law (UStG §14, §19 Kleinunternehmer, GoBD).

## Target Audience

**Primary**
- Freelancers (designers, developers, consultants)
- Solo self-employed (coaches, trainers)
- Kleinunternehmer (§19 UStG, up to €22,000 revenue)

**Secondary**
- Small craft businesses (Handwerksbetriebe)
- Service providers with few recurring clients
